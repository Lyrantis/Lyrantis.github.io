My Dissertation

This is the most important module of my entire degree: my final year project. The nature of this module allowed us to choose our own topic, determining scope with the help of our supervisor. I chose something that blended some of my key areas of interest - Physics and Virtual Reality. I wanted to create a fun, immersive VR puzzle game centred around non-Euclidean geometry. Initially, my scope was far too high - wanting a non-Newtonian physics model on top of this, I will update this page as the project is developed, so stay tuned! It is ran through to February 2026.
